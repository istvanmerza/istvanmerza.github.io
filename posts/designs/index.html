Designing stuff... isn't for me

May. 26, 2025

I created some designs for this site during development, although they didn't make it to the current version, I thought it would be a good idea to keep them around somewhere...

Inline SVG Logo "admi_"

This logo was on the front page until 2025. It should represent a sysadmin typing "admin" as username to a login prompt. The "mi" highlight represents my initials as well the site. Blinking cursor stuff requires some custom CSS I abandoned by now.

My Head

I converted an old lowres photo of me into ascii then tried to use it on the site as logo... It was a really bad idea and a real pain to work with :) Dark/Light theme, fixed font size, whitespace problems to name a few... Eventually got converted to SVG but even that could not help it ... so it got abandoned... The very first front page version even had Terminator red dot eyes, which completely threw off the CSS.
